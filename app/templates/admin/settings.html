Daily auto-submit

At the chosen time each day, every store's request is generated from current counts and submitted to Unleashed automatically. Drafts a manager has hand-edited are submitted as-is (overrides respected); stores with nothing below par are skipped; a cancelled request means that store is skipped for the day.
